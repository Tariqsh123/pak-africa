About Pakistan Africa Economic Council

The first and only organization licensed by SECP under section 42 as a non-profit Public Limited Company.

PAEC stands at the forefront of a transformative era in Pakistan-Africa relations, leveraging economic synergies, institutional linkages, and people-to-people connections to drive sustainable development and mutual prosperity. With headquarters in Karachi and strategic partnerships across Africa, we facilitate high-level policy dialogue, trade delegations, and investment matchmaking.

Islamabad Chapter

PAEC's capital hub driving policy advocacy, parliamentary engagement, and diplomatic coordination with African missions. Led by Abdul Wajid, the chapter focuses on trade facilitation and high-level government liaison.

Trade & Investment Delegations

PAEC organizes annual trade missions to Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, and Rwanda. In 2025, we facilitated 40+ B2B meetings and secured $120M in export deals.

Youth & Skills Development

PAEC's partnership with African vocational institutes and Pakistani universities offers exchange programs, technical training, and joint certification in agri-tech, IT, and renewable energy.
